Shelter of Four - Sukkahville 2015 @ Nathan Phillips Square

2nd Place & People's Choice Award

Sukkahville, as described by the Kehilla Residential Programmes's official competition site, is an international design competition that challenges entrants to re-imagine the Sukkah. The project, designed in conjunction with fellow Ryerson Architecture student (and good friend) Deena Jamokha, was first visualized in a competition panel with some of the images below. After being confirmed as one of the final 8 entries, I spent August and much of September 2015 fabricating and pre-assembling the plywood panels. The final product was constructed on-site with handheld power tools.
