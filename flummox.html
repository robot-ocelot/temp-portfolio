Flummox @ Pandemonium

TODO 2016 People's Choice Award

The piece was located at a record store, so vinyl records were re-purposed as the primary construction material. The records were heat formed and attached to a steel frame via laser-cut vellum connections. Flummox was collaboratively designed, fabricated, and constructed by [R]ed[U]x Lab members.
